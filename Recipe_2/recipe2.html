Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite pizza toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Cornmeal (for dusting)

Instructions:

  • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Sprinkle cornmeal on a pizza peel or the back of a baking sheet.
  • Transfer the rolled-out dough to the prepared peel or baking sheet.
  • Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  • Add your favorite pizza toppings on top of the cheese.
  • Drizzle a little olive oil over the toppings.
  • Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone in the oven, if using, or place the baking sheet directly in the oven.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  • Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  • Serve and enjoy!
